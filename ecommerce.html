An online store retailer with intuitive design, three-step checkout and worldwide shipping features.
Duration Ongoing

Type of project Online Electronic Marketplace

About the project

An online niche retailer with a worldwide shipping, multi-warehouse support, AI-powered content personalization, loyalty program, gift vouchers, special offers and certificates, as well as easy-to-pay checkout system in just three clicks.

Challenges

Our client approached us with a request to build a customized web retail platform from the ground up. We were required to create a highly secure and scalable infrastructure on Amazon Web Services, incorporate user-friendly features such as loyalty programs, gift vouchers, and return management, and seamlessly integrate a Magento 2 payment system with a three-step checkout process that includes detailed fraud detection with customized rules. Additionally, we implemented features that enable customers to earn and redeem loyalty points, and utilized AI-powered content personalization with multi-warehouse support to ensure smooth global shipping.

Project screens

First step

We encountered various difficulties because of the significant need for customization and extensive load testing on the platform itself.
01 CMS

We have created a module for Magento that simplifies and accelerates the process of styling CMS pages, enabling faster and more efficient creation and customization of landing pages.

02 Content

We have decided to enhance the personalization of the customer's content by incorporating algorithms that display goods based on their previous search history.

03 Support

We established a reliable infrastructure that incorporates backup policies and failover mechanisms to ensure that the store runs smoothly and securely, without requiring extra support.

04 Shipping

We have developed a system that supports multiple warehouses to make shipping faster and more convenient for both the retailer and the customers. This system automatically determines the availability and location of products.

05 Templates

We have developed a Magento module that offers pre-defined templates and customization flexibility, making it easier and quicker for our client to make changes to the catalog. This allows them to easily style CMS pages and make updates without any hassle.
