Integrating IoT technology into the property rental industry
Duration Ongoing

About the project

A company based in America that focuses on creating self-touring applications requested our assistance in improving the usability and performance of their mobile and web application.

Challenges

To keep pace with the rapid expansion of the short-term rental industry globally, the company has been making significant investments in their real estate management software, including the addition of new features and booking channel integrations. However, to cope with the surge of new clients and maintain their dominant market position, they decided to seek additional support to meet their growing demands for property management software development.

Property Listings

First step

We encountered various difficulties because of the significant need for customization and extensive load testing on the platform itself.
01 Business analytics

Our team of business analysts developed use cases, user personas, and analyzed industry trends to establish the redesign process.

02 Design

A group of designers created several mockups for both web and mobile versions of the application.

03 Development process

To expedite the release of the application, we developed a real estate application within a tight deadline.

We have integrated several travel meta-search engines into the vacation rental management software and plan to continue expanding our client's business by adding more travel booking partners.
