Medical Center App (NDA)

A Multiplatform Application for Remote Healthcare
Duration 24+ months
Team size 4 Developers
Type of project Medical Center App

About the project

Our client in this instance was a startup company based in America. Their goal was to revolutionize hospital wristbands by incorporating advanced features that would streamline hospital operations, enhance patient comfort, and ensure secure identification both within and outside of medical facilities.

Challenges

The startup that the customer launched had a goal of transforming hospital by integrating advanced features that could simplify hospital processes, enhance patient comfort, and allow for secure identification within and outside of medical facilities.

01 Fast development

Project result

The benefits of the platform include secure and uninterrupted transfer of vital data, which enables users to access an all-in-one solution integrated with clinical decision-making.

Additionally, the platform offers innovative patient identification, combined with an affordable solution for continuous use in various patient categories, including those with dementia and postoperative conditions.

These features result in a higher quality of care, supported by continuous 24/7 monitoring, and improved timeliness and quality of healthcare services provided by the provider.
